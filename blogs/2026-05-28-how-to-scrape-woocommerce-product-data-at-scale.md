If you're learning how to scrape WooCommerce product data, one of the first things you'll notice is how much store setups can vary. Different themes, custom fields, product variations, and anti-bot protections can all affect how product information is loaded and how easy it is to extract. In some stores, the data is available directly in the HTML.
