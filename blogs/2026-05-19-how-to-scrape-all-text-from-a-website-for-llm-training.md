Artificial Intelligence (AI) is quickly becoming part of everyday life, and the demand for custom language models (LLMs) is growing with it. One important step in training large language models (LLMs) is gathering a significant amount of text data. In this article, we will show how to automate text collection from an entire website using LLM web scraping techniques.
